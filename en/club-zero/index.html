Club Zero

Hello, Fresh Blood.

Club Zero is a subsidiary of our Fund with a dedicated operations team. Club Zero is an invite-only social club where only existing members can tap new members, and all applications need to be approved by a panel. The goal is to curate a community in which young entrepreneurial talent thrives. 

The Club

Club Zero strives to build a hub for connections, collaborations, and innovations. We identify and bring together some of China’s best young talent and provide them with the community and resources to create. Housed at Q Club in the heart of Shanghai, we provide a space to meet, think, and explore together. The Club is governed by a Council of seven members. Councilmembers are young talents that have made their marks in their respective fields.

Resident Mentors

Industry leaders in the technology, investment, and business worlds sit on Club Zero’s Resident Mentor Board for six months. They participate in the Club’s events and mentor members who wish to go into the industry, forming bonds and forging connections between the current generation of China’s leaders and the next.

Exclusive and Public Events

Club Zero has a thoughtfully curated events list that includes members-only affairs and activities available to prospective members as well. The events are hosted by the Club or by its members, including product launch events, industry panels, art private viewing sessions, and idea workshops.

Grants

The Initium Fund provides Club Zero members with grants to kickstart their new initiatives. Grants are donations from the Fund through our management and performance fees. This can be a grant for a research topic, angel investment in a startup, or commission for creative pieces. The Fund also helps incubate and accelerate these projects evaluated based on feasibility, originality, and social impact.
