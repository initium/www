The Initium Fund

We write the first check to innovators.

Built and operated by students, we provide founders with a strong network of investors, mentors, peers, a China market, and a $5,000 check.

Apply for Funding Partner with Us

From Zero to One, with Us by Your Side.

As your first investor, we are here to help, not to screw you up with capital.

A Check

We will provide you with a $2,000 to $5,000 check (or CNY equivalent) to support your endeavor. Instead of requiring you to incorporate immediately, we will invest through a Simple Agreement for Future Equity and convert to equity only when you go through a future round of fundraising.

A Team

We want you to be successful. We offer you support and encouragement from day zero, and will be with you every step of the way. We will discuss your idea, iterate the product, and face future investors together. We strive to use our resources to help you grow fast and steady. We are your team.

A Friend Group

As your peers, our management and investment partners are your new friends. From discussing your product to talking about your life, academics and future, we will always be right there, available 24/7. We would love to have you as your friend and go down the path of discovery together.

A Community

Through our Campus Partner network across North America, we are building the largest inter-collegiate Chinese entrepreneurship community. You can find like-minded peers here. We believe in the power of peers, and can't wait to see you do great things with all the people here.

A Platform

Partnering up with premier angel and venture funds in China and Silicon Valley, we are building the Initium Coalition to better support aspiring Chinese entrepreneurs. When you are ready to raise capitals, your project will cut the line and arrive directly at a partner's desk.

A Future

China is an enormous market with tremendous opportunities and unlimited possibilities. This may be your future after graduation. We are working with relevant authorities to provide guidance and favorable policies to push you closer to success.

Application Process

Product Requirement

We normally invest in products that are either in the ideation phase or have already built a Minimum Viable Product. This means the applicant has already done some research into the idea, and has established a valid demand. We do not require the product to have any sort of operating data, but applicants must be able to clearly state what the product is, the total addressable market and the user profile.

Eligibility

At least one current student or recent graduate (within last 6 months) must be present in the founding team. We will prioritize applications from juniors, seniors and masters students but will place equal considerations on freshmen and sophomores. For freshmen and sophomores, we recommend looking into The Thiel Fellowship as it may be a better fit for your endeavor.

Application Materials

  1. A presentation. The presentation should be concise and provide a clear big picture - what you are pitching, why it works, how it works, and how it fits the grand scheme of things.
  2. A prospectus. The prospectus should be detailed. In addition to all contents in the presentation, please include any research and survey data you may have that supports your product having a validated demand.
  3. Current product (if applicable). If you have already started working on your product, please provide us with your current version of the product. If there is a Minimum Viable Product, please submit a video demo or live demo.
If you have any supporting materials that you think may help our deliberation, please send an email to bp@initium.fund with the subject [your name] Application Supporting Materials after you submit the application form.

Application Timeline

After you sucessfully submit the application, we will follow the following timeline and procedures in evaluating your application:
  • Within 3 business days: an Investment Partner and a Campus Partner will compete the initial screening and get back to you with initial feedbacks.
  • Within 5 business days: we will sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement with you. The application will complete our internal process. Our investment team will begin Due Dilligence.
  • Between 5-10 business days: Members of our investment team will meet with you either in-person or online, and continue our Due Dilligence process.
  • Within 12 business days: Our Investment Committee will meet with you either in-erson or online. The Investment Committee will complete the vetting process and produce a voting result.
  • Within 15 business days: You will sign the SAFE, and we will write you the check.
When we face a backlog, we will amend the aforementioned timeline and procedures as we see fit and communicate with applicants promptly.

Frequently-Asked Questions

Basic Information (4)

What is the Initium Fund?
The Initium Fund is a student-operated venture fund that focuses on overseas Chinese student entrepreneurs. In other words, we invest in our peer's entrepreneurship endeavors.
Why do you exist?
Traditional angel and venture funds normally invest after the product has already generated revenue, yet campus incubators are unable to provide tailored resources and mentorship. For student entrepreneurs, it is essential that we provide support and courage from day zero. We are certain that universities have the highest density of intelligent, able and innovative people in the planet, so we set out to create this fund that supports, encourages, and foster student founders and aspiring investors.
Who do you invest in?
We invest in student founders that are either current students or have recently graduated within 6 months. We will prioritize upperclassmen and masters applicants but underclassmen will receive equal considerations. We invest in projects that are in the ideation phase or have an MVP. Applicants should be able to clearly state the details of the project.
What's your team composition?
Our executive board is currently consisted of five undergraduates at Columbia University. We are rapidly expanding the Campus Partner network in North America as well. A team of advisors holding high positions in venture funds in China provides us with guidance and suggestions for our day-to-day operations.

Investment Process (4)

What is your investment process like?
Our whole investment process usually completes within three weeks. You have to complete an online application form and submit a presentation and a prospectus. We will provide initial feedbacks within one week. If we are interesting in funding you, we will complete two interviews with you either in-person or online.
When should I start applying?
As long as you have an idea and have already invested some time into it, we would love to hear it. If you can clearly and coherently tell us what your idea is, you would definitely fit the bill. If you are worried that your idea is not mature enough, an application would connect us to you and help you perfect your idea together. We are here every step of the way.
If I get funded, what's next?
We will use an instrument called Simple Agreement for Future Equity to invest in you. SAFE is an open-sourced agreement published by the famed Y Combinator. If you decide to take the money, we will issue a $2,000 to $5,000 check. You can appropriate the fund freely with reasonable cause, but are required to comply with a financial audit as we see fit.
If I don't get funded, can I apply again?
We welcome you to reapply anytime you want. The new application should have some material difference comparing to the previous one.

Investment Details (4)

What is a SAFE?
SAFE is the most founder-friendly investment agreement. It is a convertible note. After we complete our investment, we do not hold any equities. We only hold equities after you grow and complete a valued round of fundraising.
What is a convertible note?
A convertible note is a financial instrument that converts to equity once certain conditions are met. Although the convertible note we issue will most likely not become your personal debts, there are exceptional situations where you would be liable to the note (such as gross negligence). We want you to be serious about this investment, as we are investing in you in good faith.
When will the SAFE be converted?
We will work together to come up with a reasonable schedule for SAFE conversion. Normally, we will convert to equity when you undergo your next round of fundraising, with a discount rate of 20%.
Where will you invest in?
Right now, we have Investment Partners in New York, Boston, Philadelphia, San Francisco and Los Angeles. However, we invest all across the United States and Canada.

Investment Team (3)

Who are on your investment team?
Our investment team is decentralized in major cities in the United States. Members are full-time students at a variety of universities. They are also our Campus Partners. Members of our investment team come from diverse background with incredible past experience, and they each has a unique story to tell. We have apsiring founders, investors, bankers, engineers, hackers, philosophers and historians on-board. We are an energetic team.
What do Campus Partners do?
Our Campus Partners are our official representatitves at their home campus. Each partner can collect, recommend, and vet projects. Partners are also responsible for conducting in-person interviews and meetings with applicants. Campus Partners are members of the Investment Committee in which they are involved in making a final investment decision. Additionally, by working with our Venture Partners and our marketing team, partners will host entrepreneurship events at their home campus to increase our presence.

The Campus Partner team is essential in our mission. As a newly-founded team ourselves, we are also in the startup mode. Partners will be involved in our day-to-day operations and also strategic planning, propelling us to move forward together.
How can I join the team?
We will start our next round of Campus Partner application soon. Meanwhile, if you are interested in what we do, or want to discuss something with us, please not hesitate to email us at contact@initium.fund.
